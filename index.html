About Me

Hello, I'm Rudraksh, a passionate photographer and filmmaker who believes in the power of transforming ideas into impactful visual experiences. Always in the process of learning something new, creating something new, and adding value wherever I can, I mix creativity, emotion, and storytelling into visuals that capture a moment yet evoke feelings which time cannot take away. Most of my techniques in composition, color tone, editing, and visual storytelling have been self-taught through hands-on experimentation, curiosity, and years of practice. Each project I take on is an opportunity to experiment with perspective, light, and emotion, aiming to create visuals that resonate beyond the screen.
